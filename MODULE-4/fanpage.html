Jungle Fever

The hive-mind known as Jungle ascended from the stars in 2013, from an unknown planet around a distant star. Earth's greatest defenses have proven completely harmless to the Jungle entity, sometimes even seeming to feed its creative flow into dangerous, concentrated beams of pure funk capable of rendering a crowd of thousands immediately catatonic upon exposure.
